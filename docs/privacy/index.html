Privacy Policy for TubeDeck

Effective Date: 8th July 2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Your Google account ID (for identifying your session)
  • Your YouTube subscription list (via secure Google API access)

We do not collect:

  • Your emails
  • Your password or any credentials
  • Any sensitive or personally identifiable data beyond what is required for functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We only use your data to:

  • Show your subscribed YouTube channels
  • Allow you to bookmark, tag, and organize content across platforms
  • Enhance your personalized experience

We do not share your data with third parties, use your data for advertising or analytics, or post on your behalf.

3. Data Security

All communication with Google APIs is handled over secure HTTPS connections. No data is stored on our servers unless explicitly stated (e.g., bookmarks stored locally on your device).

4. Your Control

You can:

  • Revoke Google access at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • Uninstall the app to stop data access entirely

5. Third-party Services

This app uses:

  • Google Sign-In for authentication
  • YouTube Data API to access your subscriptions

Usage is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and YouTube Terms of Service.
